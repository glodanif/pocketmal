Pocket MAL is an unofficial Android client for a popular anime knowledge base myanimelist.net. As the original website, this application will provide you with an ability to review detailed information about any anime or manga


And the key feature of myanimelist is tracking - you will never lose the list and progress of watched anime and read manga
